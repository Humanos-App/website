# Environment Rules (Vercel-Specific)
# Prevents preview deployments from being indexed

## Environment Detection
- Vercel env variable: VERCEL_ENV
- Production value: "production"
- Preview value: "preview"
- Development value: "development"

---

## RULE: env.preview_must_noindex
Severity: CRITICAL

Preview and development deployments must be noindex,nofollow.

Applies to:
- Environments: preview, development
- Content types: text/html

Check:
- meta[name='robots'] must contain "noindex" AND "nofollow"

Why: Prevents duplicate content issues and keeps preview URLs out of Google.

---

## RULE: env.prod_must_be_indexable
Severity: CRITICAL

Production pages must NOT have noindex.

Applies to:
- Environment: production
- Content types: text/html

Check:
- meta[name='robots'] must NOT contain "noindex"

Why: Ensures your production site can actually be crawled and indexed.
